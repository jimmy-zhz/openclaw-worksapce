## 6. Risk Register

### High Priority Risks:
1. **Policy Change Risk:** MPNP or PGWP eligibility criteria modification during study period
   - Probability: Medium
   - Impact: High
   - Mitigation: Regular policy monitoring, contingency planning

2. **Program Completion Risk:** Academic challenges affecting diploma completion
   - Probability: Low-Medium
   - Impact: High
   - Mitigation: Academic support utilization, proactive study management

3. **Family Separation Risk:** Extended separation affecting personal well-being
   - Probability: High
   - Impact: Medium
   - Mitigation: Regular communication, clear reunification timeline

4. **Financial Risk:** Insufficient funds for immigration process or family support
   - Probability: Medium
   - Impact: High
   - Mitigation: Budget planning, potential part-time work options

### Medium Priority Risks:
5. **Job Market Risk:** Post-graduation employment challenges in Manitoba
6. **Language Proficiency Risk:** English requirements for immigration points
7. **Documentation Risk:** Missing or insufficient application materials
