## Risk Assessment Scale

### Probability Scale:
- **Very High (VH):** >80% likelihood
- **High (H):** 61-80% likelihood
- **Medium (M):** 41-60% likelihood
- **Low (L):** 21-40% likelihood
- **Very Low (VL):** ≤20% likelihood

### Impact Scale:
- **Critical (C):** Project failure or abandonment
- **High (H):** Major schedule/cost overrun (>30%)
- **Medium (M):** Moderate schedule/cost impact (10-30%)
- **Low (L):** Minor impact, manageable
- **Very Low (VL):** Negligible impact

## Active Risks

### High Priority Risks (Require Immediate Attention)

#### R-001: Immigration Policy Change Risk
- **Description:** MPNP or PGWP eligibility criteria modification during study period
- **Probability:** Medium (M)
- **Impact:** High (H)
- **Risk Score:** M×H = Medium-High
- **Status:** Active
- **Owner:** Jimmy
- **Trigger:** Government policy announcements, IRCC updates
- **Mitigation Strategies:**
  1. Regular monitoring of IRCC and MPNP policy updates
  2. Subscription to immigration policy newsletters
  3. Consultation with immigration consultants quarterly
  4. Contingency planning for alternative pathways
- **Contingency Plan:** If policy changes adversely affect eligibility, explore:
  - Other provincial nominee programs
  - Express Entry pathway optimization
  - Program extension or additional qualifications

#### R-002: Academic Program Completion Risk
- **Description:** Challenges in completing AI diploma program within expected timeframe
- **Probability:** Low-Medium (L-M)
- **Impact:** High (H)
- **Risk Score:** (L-M)×H = Medium
- **Status:** Active
- **Owner:** Jimmy
- **Trigger:** Academic performance below expectations, personal circumstances
- **Mitigation Strategies:**
  1. Regular academic progress monitoring
  2. Utilization of university support services (tutoring, counseling)
  3. Time management and study schedule optimization
  4. Early identification of challenging subjects
- **Contingency Plan:** If academic challenges arise:
  - Request program extension if available
  - Consider reduced course load with immigration implications assessment
  - Explore alternative qualifying programs

#### R-003: Family Separation Risk
- **Description:** Extended family separation affecting personal well-being and family dynamics
- **Probability:** High (H)
- **Impact:** Medium (M)
- **Risk Score:** H×M = Medium-High
- **Status:** Active
- **Owner:** Jimmy
- **Trigger:** Extended separation beyond expected timeline, family emergencies
- **Mitigation Strategies:**
  1. Regular communication schedule (daily/weekly calls)
  2. Clear timeline communication with family
  3. Financial planning for family visits if feasible

#### R-004: Financial Resource Risk

- **Probability:** Medium (M)

- **Risk Score:** M×H = Medium-High
- **Status:** Active
- **Owner:** Jimmy
