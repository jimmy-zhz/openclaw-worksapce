## Decision Log

### Strategic Decisions

#### D-001: Immigration Pathway Selection
- **Decision Date:** 2023
- **Decision:** Pursue Canadian immigration via Manitoba Provincial Nominee Program (MPNP) through study pathway
- **Status:** ✅ Approved/Implemented
- **Decision Maker:** Jimmy
- **Alternatives Considered:**
  1. Express Entry Federal Skilled Worker Program
  2. Other Provincial Nominee Programs (BC, Ontario, etc.)
  3. Family sponsorship (not applicable)
- **Rationale:**
  - MPNP offers relatively faster processing for Manitoba graduates
  - Study pathway provides Canadian education and work experience
  - Lower points requirement compared to Express Entry
  - Alignment with career transition to AI/software field
- **Supporting Data:**
  - MPNP success rates for international students
  - Manitoba job market growth in tech sector
  - Comparative analysis of immigration pathways
- **Implementation:** English preparation initiated, study permit application process started
