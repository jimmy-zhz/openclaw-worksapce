## CRISP-DM Overview
CRISP-DM is a 6-phase process model for data mining projects:

1. **Business Understanding**
2. **Data Understanding**
3. **Data Preparation**
4. **Modeling**
5. **Evaluation**
6. **Deployment**

## Current Focus Areas
